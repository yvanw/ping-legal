Privacy Policy

Last updated: April 2026

Ping ("we", "our", "the app") is a social availability app. This policy explains what data we collect, how we use it, and your rights.

Data we collect

  • Phone number — used for login via SMS OTP. Supabase Auth retains it in its authentication infrastructure to validate your identity. In our own profile database, only a one-way SHA-256 hash of your number is stored, used solely for friend discovery. We never read or use the raw number ourselves beyond the sign-in flow.
  • Display name and handle — shown to people you connect with. You choose these.
  • Availability status — shared only with the circles you select. Auto-expires after the duration you set.
  • Device push token — used solely to deliver notifications to your device. Never shared.
  • Contact list (optional) — scanned locally and hashed to find friends already on Ping. Raw contact data is never sent to our servers.

How we use your data

  • To operate the app and deliver its core features
  • To send push notifications you have enabled
  • To match you with friends via phone hash comparison

We do not sell your data. We do not run ads. We do not share your data with third parties except as required to operate the service (Supabase for infrastructure).

Data storage

Your data is stored on Supabase infrastructure in the EU region (eu-west-3, Paris). Supabase is SOC 2 Type II certified.

Data retention

Your data is retained as long as your account exists. Statuses auto-expire after the duration you set. You can delete your account at any time from the Settings screen in the app, which permanently removes all your data from our servers.

Your rights

  • Access — you can see all data associated with your account in the app
  • Deletion — delete your account from Settings → Delete account
  • Portability — contact us to request a copy of your data
  • Correction — edit your profile at any time from the app

If you are located in the EU, you have additional rights under GDPR. Contact us at the address below to exercise them.

Children

Ping is not intended for users under 13 years of age. We do not knowingly collect data from children under 13.

Changes to this policy

We may update this policy from time to time. We will notify you of significant changes via the app. Continued use after changes constitutes acceptance.

Questions or data requests: pingapp.support@gmail.com
